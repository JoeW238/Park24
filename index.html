Property Overview

Elegant living in the heart of Bangkok

Nestled between Sukhumvit 22 and 24, six luxury towers rest within quiet, peaceful grounds, providing a calming, welcoming retreat within the heart of the city. The property has been thoughtfully finished, incorporating high-quality modern touches.

Boasting double-height windows which give breathtaking views over the Bangkok skyline as well as flooding the space with natural light, the majority of the lower floor is given over to the open-plan kitchen, living and dining area.

The remainder of the lower floor comprises a guest cloakroom, an understairs storage cupboard as well as a double en-suite bedroom. On the first floor there is another double en-suite bedroom as well as an open plan office area overlooking the lower living area.
