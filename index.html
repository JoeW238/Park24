Property Overview

Elegant living in the heart of Bangkok

Nestled between Sukhumvit 22 and 24, six luxury towers rest within quiet, peaceful grounds, providing a calming, welcoming retreat within the heart of the city.

The double-height windows across floors 41 and 42 give this duplex apartment breathtaking views over the Bangkok skyline as well as flooding the open-plan living area with natural light.

The remainder of the lower floor comprises a guest cloakroom, an understairs storage cupboard as well as a double en-suite bedroom. On the first floor there is another double en-suite bedroom as well as an open plan office area overlooking the lower living area.
